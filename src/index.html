Forbidden word is a simple social deduction game. Each player is randomly assigned another player to create a forbidden word for. Each player knows everybody else's word but their own. Once the game starts, there is only one goal: don't say your word before others say theirs. It's against the spirit of the to avoid talking, or giving answers that are too succinct. The game is best when everybody treats it like a normal conversation. You also can't excessively use other languages, hand gestures, or other non-verbal to communicate (using it to avoid a particular word is usually ok, but any more than a phrase and that's bad).
